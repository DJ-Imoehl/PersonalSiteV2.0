    Personal Website - Version 2.0

    Overall Function and Purpose

    I wanted the second version of my personal website to be somewhere I could advertise myself and my projects. Visitors should walk away with knowledge of who I am, what values I hold, what projects I've published, and what languages and libraries I've used. The information on my resume should almost be completely separate from the information on this website.

    This is one aspect of my original website that I really disliked. If a potential employer wants to see resume information, they could very easily ask me for a resume, or look at my LinkedIn profile.

    In addition to that, I also wanted to practice using plain HTML/CSS/JavaScript (except the Bootstrap CSS library). I haven't worked with these languages like this for a long time, and it was nice to have a refresher.
